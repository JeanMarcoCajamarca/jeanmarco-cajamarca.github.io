ABOUT.TXT

Hello, World. I'm Jean Marco Cajamarca. But you can call me Jack Karusel!

I am a video game developer focused on creating immersive gameplay systems and polished player experiences. My work spans gameplay mechanics, UI systems, and experimental indie projects.

When it involves compiling datasets, designing systems, or building side projects in game development, I bring analytical rigor and creative energy to everything I ship.

I am also a tech & business professional! Full of passion about solving real-world problems through data, strategy, and technology, I operate at the intersection of economics, business intelligence, and product thinking.

I was born in New York City, NY, in the year 2002. My father and mother were born in Ecuador and Mexico respectively. They immigrated to the United States but received no US citizenship status. Even after marriage, they were not able to receive pensions or financial assitance. All they could afford is public housing and cash to support their kids.

I attended P.S. 72 Lexington Academy where I graduated in 2013. I later attended DREAM Charter School, graduated in 2016. Throughout my high school era, I attended All Hallows High School, becoming one of the first students to graduate in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic in the year 2020.

I attended the Redemptoris Mater Seminary in Denver, CO, taking courses at the St. John Vianney Theological Seminary, from 2020 to 2022.

I am currently attending CUNY Hunter College, majoring in Economics. But how the heck am I going to blend tech stuff to Econ?
